Coding Club

Coding Club takes place every Tuesday, at the Vicksburg Middle School in room 404. It goes from 2:30 pm to 4:00 pm. We have many things to do during that time to code! Robots with arduinos, Websites with HTML, or Games with Scratch. Mrs. Harbour also gives out snacks if you would like one!

Where to learn code?

There are many resources for programming. examples include. Codecademy, Udemy, W3schools, FreeCodeCamp.org, Khanacademy, SkillShare... Also Programming is where you can get stuck. You might need help... Thats why the internet is here. To help you solve what you need. StackOverFlow is a place where developers come and ask questions that they have with a problem that they can't solve or basic questions that they still don't understand.
